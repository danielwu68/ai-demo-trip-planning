# Points of Interest (POI)

## Arizona

### Ehrenberg

#### Rest Stop
- **Name:** Arizona Welcome Center (Ehrenberg)
  - **Address:** Ehrenberg Rest Area Eb, Quartzsite, AZ 85346
  - **Phone Number(s):** (928) 927-6652 (Ehrenberg Port of Entry - often co-located or nearby)
  - **Google Map:** [https://www.google.com/maps/search/Quartzsite,+AZ+85346](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Quartzsite,+AZ+85346)
  - **Description:** This welcome center and rest area along I-10 East provides essential amenities such as parking, restrooms, and shaded seating. Visitors can find travel brochures, maps, and informational displays about Arizona. It's a convenient stop to refresh and gather information.

### Sedona

#### Park
- **Name:** Red Rock State Park
  - **Address:** 4050 Lower Red Rock Rd, Sedona, AZ 86336
  - **Phone Number(s):** (928) 282-6907
  - **Google Map:** [https://www.google.com/maps/search/4050+Lower+Red+Rock+Rd,+Sedona,+AZ+86336](https://www.google.com/maps/search/4050+Lower+Red+Rock+Rd,+Sedona,+AZ+86336)
  - **Description:** A 286-acre nature preserve and environmental education center featuring Sedona's iconic red rock formations and a stretch of Oak Creek. Offers numerous hiking trails with varying difficulty, opportunities for wildlife viewing, and educational programs. A beautiful spot for immersing in Sedona's natural beauty.

#### Landmark
- **Name:** Chapel of the Holy Cross
  - **Address:** 780 Chapel Rd, Sedona, AZ 86336
  - **Phone Number(s):** (928) 282-7545, (928) 282-4069
  - **Google Map:** [https://www.google.com/maps/search/780+Chapel+Rd,+Sedona,+AZ+86336](https://www.google.com/maps/search/780+Chapel+Rd,+Sedona,+AZ+86336)
  - **Description:** An architectural marvel built into the red rock buttes of Sedona. This Roman Catholic chapel offers a serene atmosphere for reflection and prayer, along with breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. It's a must-visit for its unique design and spiritual ambiance.
