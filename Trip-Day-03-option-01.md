# Day 3: Day Trip to Sedona

This day is dedicated to exploring the scenic beauty of Sedona. We will drive from Phoenix in the morning, visit two state parks, have lunch, and then return to Phoenix in the evening.

## 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM (2 hours)
*   **Activity:** Drive from Phoenix to Sedona
*   **Details:**
    *   **Origin:** Phoenix, AZ
    *   **Destination:** Sedona, AZ
    *   **Google Maps Link:** [https://www.google.com/maps/dir/Phoenix,+AZ/Sedona,+AZ/](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/Phoenix,+AZ/Sedona,+AZ/)
