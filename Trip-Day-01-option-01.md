# Day 1: Los Angeles to Kingman

This first day involves a 4-hour drive from Los Angeles to Kingman, Arizona. We will be leaving in the afternoon to avoid the worst of LA's traffic. After arriving in Kingman and checking into the hotel, we will have a relaxed dinner at a classic Route 66 diner.

## 3:00 PM - 7:00 PM (4 hours)
*   **Activity:** Drive from Los Angeles to Kingman
*   **Details:**
    *   **Origin:** Los Angeles, CA
    *   **Destination:** Kingman, AZ
    *   **Google Maps Link:** [https://www.google.com/maps/dir/Los+Angeles,+CA/Kingman,+AZ/](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/Los+Angeles,+CA/Kingman,+AZ/)
    *   **Notes:** The drive is approximately 230 miles. Consider the traffic when leaving LA.
